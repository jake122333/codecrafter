About Us

Welcome to the Museum of the United States, where history comes alive! Our mission is to preserve and showcase the rich cultural heritage of the United States through engaging exhibits and educational programs.

Exhibits

  • American Revolution: Explore the events and figures that shaped the birth of a nation.
  • Civil Rights Movement: Discover the struggle for equality and justice in America.
  • Innovations in Technology: A look at how American ingenuity has changed the world.
